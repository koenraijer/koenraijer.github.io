Hi! I'm Koen,
a soon-to-be medical doctor.

I have been learning programming. You are looking at my latest work (in progress).

Technology determines more and more of how we live our lives. Undoubtably so, the medical field will not be any different. Instead of standing on the medical sideline, I am going to be right there at the intersection.

I am fascinated and a little worried by the future of mental health.

This ever-so-rapidly changing world, is marked by an astonishing rise in the amount of people that suffer from mental illness. In areas where technology is harming us, modern healthcare should devise ways to reduce the strain of technology. At the same time, many very real technological innovations will be of great benefit.

I am a generalist with one foot in both the world of medicine and technology. Let me think with you!

Looking at a problem from various angles plays a crucial part in solving it, and I can help you make it come together.
